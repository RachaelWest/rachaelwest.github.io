My Story

My interest in web development began in late 2019 during the Covid-19 pandemic when I decided to change careers. I had previously worked in journalism and communication roles for over 10 years but, after some soul searching, realised I needed a new challenge.

My past roles involved writing content for digital products, including websites, and I have always been interested in how it all fits together. I was fortunate to work closely with designers and developers in the past and had the opportunity to watch them work.

I decided I wanted to do more than just write content for websites - I wanted to help build the product itself. I set out to learn the fundamentals of front-end development and haven't looked back.

Since then, I have completed a CIW-certified course from The Training Room and ten Codecademy courses (and counting!). I have also completed a certified course for Responsive Web Design and recently started Front End Development Libraries, both from freeCodeCamp.

I also started the Frontend Developer Career Path by code-learning platform Scrimba a few months ago and continue to learn as much as I can.
