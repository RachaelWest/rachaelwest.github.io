My Story

I am a communications specialist and former journalist with over 12 years of experience who is now looking for a new challenge.

My past roles involved writing content for digital products, including websites, and I was fortunate to work closely with designers and developers on various products.

I have always had an interest in working across digital platforms and activiely seek out new opportunities to lean new skills.

I have completed several online courses about the fundamentals of front-end development, SEO, responsive design, and programming languages including JavaScript.

I am also keen to learn about fundamental digital marketing principles, audience research, SEO and competitor analysis, and how to create successful campaigns. I also have an interest in search engine optimisation and often read the latest developments in this area from various sources including Search Engine Journal, SEMRush, and Hrefs.

I continue to learn as much as I can and look forward to the next challenge where I can learn and apply new skills to a high standard and advance my professional development.
